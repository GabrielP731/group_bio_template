Team Rules

Scrum Values

Our team follows Scrum values of commitment, courage, focus, openness, and respect to ensure effective collaboration throughout the project.

Sprint Schedule

  • Monday 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM: Sprint planning — plan tasks for the sprint and review completed work.
  • Wednesday 7:30 PM - 9:00 PM: Progress review — discuss obstacles and adjust approach if needed.
  • Monday - Wednesday: Focused execution on assigned tasks.
  • Wednesday - Friday: Completing and refining assigned tasks.
  • Weekends: Generally free unless additional time is needed to stay on track.

Deadlines & Accountability

  • All assigned tasks must be completed by Friday at 6:00 PM.
  • If a member cannot finish by Friday, they are expected to continue working over the weekend.
  • All team members must communicate progress by sending a message before 6:00 PM on Friday if they encounter issues, delays, or need assistance.

Conflict Resolution

  • If a team member is uncooperative or consistently fails to contribute, the team will first address the issue directly and respectfully as a group.
  • If the behavior continues, responsibilities may be redistributed and the issue will be escalated to the instructor if necessary.
